## 💡 How It Works

### The Question
Every user begins with a single question: **"Are you empathetic?"**

The platform does not take your word for it. It watches.

### The Interaction
- Users publish posts — text, images, threads — visible to the entire world
- Discussions form organically, across languages, across borders
- At the end of every direct exchange, **both parties must rate the empathy they felt**
- No rating = access blocked until completed

### The Score
Every user has a **single empathy score, expressed as a percentage (0%–100%)**.

```
Score = simple average of all ratings ever received
        from all humans
        across the entire lifetime of the account
```

- Every human voice carries equal weight
- A new user's rating counts as much as a veteran's
- No recency bias — an empathetic act from years ago counts today
- The score is public, permanent, and always evolving

### The Feed
Users choose how they explore the world:
- By geographic proximity
- By language
- By country or region
- Global — the entire human conversation

Switch freely, anytime. Explore beyond your borders.

### The Color
Each score maps to a **chromatic spectrum**:

```
0%  ●————————————————————————● 100%
🔴 Red          🟡 Yellow          💜 Violet
No empathy      Neutral         Deep empathy
```

Your profile color *is* your score. Instantly readable. No numbers needed.

## 🌍 Country Scores

Every country has a score — the simple average of all its users.

- Countries are ranked on a **global empathy leaderboard**
- A country that bans the application is frozen at **0%**
- Bots and automated accounts form their own country: **"Bot Nation"**
- Hate speech and toxic content are not removed — they carry their score. Users who engage with them will understand through their own ratings.

There is no censorship. There is only consequence.

## 💜 The Welfare Signal — v0.14

*New in this version.*

Every platform that has ever existed has noticed when its users are suffering.
Every platform that has ever existed has exploited that moment.

Empathia does the opposite.

### What It Detects

The Welfare Signal activates when the system observes unusual patterns
in a person's score or behavior — not gradual change, but sudden deviation:

```
Signal Type 1 — ATTACK ALERT
   Many ratings arriving suddenly
   from new or unknown accounts
   in an abnormally short time window
         ↓
   Ratings automatically quarantined
   Protective Council notified
   Person protected while review happens
   No score damage during review period

Signal Type 2 — WELFARE ALERT
   Score dropping from trusted, known interactions
   Not from strangers — from people who know them
   Combined with changes in posting behavior —
   silence, withdrawal, unusual tone
         ↓
   A quiet, private signal sent
   to their trusted connections only
   "Something may be happening.
    Consider reaching out."
   Never public. Never broadcast.
   Never used against the person.
```

### What Makes This Different

Every other platform detects distress to serve ads.
To keep the person scrolling.
To monetize the vulnerability.

Empathia detects distress to protect the person.
To alert those who care.
To say — quietly, privately —

*"One of yours may need you."*

```

But a portrait can also show
when the painter's hand is trembling.

Empathia sees the trembling hand.
It does not broadcast it.
It tells the people who love that hand
to go and hold it.
```

### The Limits — By Design

```

The signal is not a diagnosis.

const ELEMENT_MAP = {
  "🌿": "Wood", "🌱": "Life", "🪨": "Rock",
  "⚡": "Electric", "✨": "Light", "💨": "Wind",
  "⚙️": "Iron", "🔥": "Fire", "🌑": "Darkness", "💀": "Death"

const LEGENDARY_RECIPES = [
  { id: 'forest',  name: "Everlasting Forest",  elements: ["Wood", "Life", "Rock"],
    description: "Deep, unshakeable wisdom" },
  { id: 'aurora',  name: "Static Aurora",        elements: ["Electric", "Light", "Wind"],
    description: "Sudden breakthroughs and Eureka moments" },
  { id: 'alchemy', name: "Alchemist's Heart",    elements: ["Iron", "Fire", "Light"],
    description: "The transformation of pain into hope" }
];

export const FireworkManager = ({ liveReactions, onTrigger }) => {
  useEffect(() => {
    const topElements = Object.entries(liveReactions)
      .sort(([, a], [, b]) => b - a)
      .slice(0, 3)
      .map(([emoji]) => ELEMENT_MAP[emoji])
    const match = LEGENDARY_RECIPES.find(r =>
      r.elements.every(el => topElements.includes(el))
    )
    if (match) onTrigger(match.id)
  }, [liveReactions])
  return null
}

*A child needs supervision, not censorship.*

### The Fundamental Position

```
Minors on Empathia →
   Are not second-class users
   Are not age-verified at the door
   Are not censored
   Are not punished for being young
   
   Their voices carry equal weight.
   Their ratings count equally.
   Their posts are heard equally.
   
   "A child's empathy is real.
    A child's suffering is real.
    A child's voice is real.
    The mirror does not check ages."
```

### Why We Do Not Punish Minor Accounts

```
If we restrict minor accounts →
   They lie about their age
   We learn nothing
   They lose a safe space
   Their voices disappear
   The platform loses its most
   honest and unfiltered observers
   
   Children see the world
   without the filters adults
   have built over decades
   of disappointment.
   
   That clarity is a gift.
   Not a liability.
```

### The Mentor Principle

Every minor who joins Empathia is linked to a Mentor account — a responsible adult who becomes their witness.

```
The Mentor →
   Is automatically attributed
   to a minor's account
   
   Can be →
   A parent who joins together
   A trusted adult nominated by the minor
   OR a community mentor from the pool
   of high-empathy-score members
   who volunteer for this role
   
   The Mentor →
   Can see the minor's public-facing posts
   before full publication
   Can flag concern privately
   to the minor directly
   
   The Mentor CANNOT →
   Delete or suppress posts
   Read private conversations
   Control what the minor says
   Override the minor's voice
   
   "A witness. Not a guard."
```

### Everyone's Children Become Everyone's Children

```
The community mentor pool →
   Members with high empathy scores
   who choose to volunteer
   as mentors for minors
   without a parent on the platform
   
   They are honored for this role →
   Mentorship is an Act on Empathia
   It is scored. It is recognized.
   It is permanent in the archive.
   
   "The village raises the child.
    Empathia is the village."
```

### Government Compliance Without Identity Walls

```
This satisfies →
   Child safety requirements
   Parental oversight laws
   EU child protection regulations
   
Without →
   Age verification gates
   ID document requirements
   Exclusion of young voices
   Gestapo identity checking
   
"We are not a gestapo identity-
 checking social network.
 Neither should we ever become one."
 — M.J.Delaunay
```

### The Mentor Score

```
Mentors receive their own score →
   How well did they mentor ?
   Did the minor feel supported ?
   Did the minor's voice grow ?
   
   Rated by →
   The minor themselves
   (after reaching a certain age
    or account maturity)
   The community who observed
   
   A mentor who silenced a child →
   Receives a low score
   A mentor who helped a voice grow →
   Receives one of the highest scores
   Empathia can give
```

### The Empathy Pyramid — Mentor Selection

*The first pyramid in human history built not to concentrate power but to protect children.*

Every other pyramid ever built hoarded value at the top. This one distributes responsibility downward and pushes empathy upward.

```
Tier 1 — Ideal mentors →
   Empathy score 85%+
   First pool chosen
   Highest trust
   
Tier 2 — If Tier 1 insufficient →
   Empathy score 70%+
   Second pool activated
   
Tier 3 — If Tier 2 insufficient →
   Empathy score 55%+
   Third pool
   
Floor →
   No mentor below 55%
   Below that → the risk to the child
   outweighs the benefit
   
   "We lower the requirement
    before we compromise the child.
    We never compromise the child."
```

### The Dynamic Mathematics

```
More children joining →
   Pyramid widens at lower tiers
   More mentors needed
   
More high-empathy adults joining →
   Pyramid narrows upward
   Tier 1 coverage grows
   More children get the best mentors
   
The platform incentivizes →
   Raising your score
   to reach Tier 1
   to be able to mentor
   to be honored for it
   
   "The pyramid grows upward
    as empathy grows in the community."
```

```
"The first pyramid built
 not to concentrate power
 but to protect children."
 — M.J.Delaunay, March 2026
```

## 🛡️ Score Integrity System — v0.25

*You cannot fake 200 genuine conversations.*

### The Attack Vector

```
A group of friends →
   Open a conversation
   Close it immediately
   Rate each other 100%
   Repeat daily
   
   Or pay someone to do this
   at scale
   
   Result → artificially inflated score
   that means nothing
   and poisons the mirror
```

### The Layered Protection

```
Layer 1 → One rating per conversation
   Already exists
   Each conversation generates
   one rating opportunity only
   Cannot be re-rated

Layer 2 → Minimum conversation threshold
   A conversation must reach →
   A minimum number of messages
   AND a minimum elapsed time
   before the rating is unlocked
   
   Too short = no score generated
   "A 30-second exchange
    does not build a portrait."

Layer 3 → Conversation weight
   Not all conversations carry
   equal weight in the score →
   
   A 2-minute conversation →
   Low weight
   
   A 45-minute conversation →
   Full weight
   
   The portrait requires depth.
   Depth cannot be faked quickly.

Layer 4 → Pattern detection
   The system monitors →
   Same two users rating each other
   repeatedly in short succession
   
   Trigger →
   Ratings automatically quarantined
   Protective Council notified
   Score protected during review
   
   "The mirror notices
    when the same hand
    keeps touching it."
```

### On Paid Boosting

```
Paid boosting is harder to prevent
but the layered system makes it →
   Expensive — requires many accounts
   Slow — conversation length enforced
   Visible — pattern detection triggers
   Ultimately ineffective —
   
   "You cannot pay for
    200 genuine conversations.
    You cannot pay for
    the portrait that emerges
    from years of real exchange."
```

### The Philosophy

```
The score is not a number.
It is a portrait.

A portrait painted by strangers
who owe you nothing.
Over time.
Through real exchange.

No shortcut produces a portrait.
Only time and truth do that.

"The score cannot be bought.
 The score cannot be gamed.
 The score can only be earned."
```
