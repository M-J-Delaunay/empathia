What Empathia is

🌍

A global forum

Open to the entire world. Every voice equal. Every language welcome. Explore by proximity, country, or dive into the full human conversation.

💜

A living score

Every interaction ends with a mutual empathy rating. Your score — expressed as a percentage — is the simple average of every human who has ever spoken with you.

🗺️

Empathia Streets

A living map of the world's empathic geography. Museums, parks, gathering spaces — every place scored by those who felt something there.

🔥

The Acts

Not a news platform. An acts platform. Where traditional media documents what breaks, Empathia documents what repairs.

🌐

Country scores

Every nation has a score. Every city. Every institution. A mirror held up to the world — built by the people who live in it.

🛡️

Cannot be bought

Finance will try to buy empathy. Empathia is built so that it cannot be sold. AGPL-3.0. No ads. No government API. No gatekeeping.
